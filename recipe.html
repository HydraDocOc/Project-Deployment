Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 40 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • FOR MASALA PASTE:
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • tbsp butter
  • 5 cloves
  • 2 pods cardamom
  • 2 onion, sliced
  • 2 clove garlic
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 3 tomato, sliced
  • 15 cashew
  • FOR CURRY:
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 chilli
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • ¾ tsp coriander powder
  • ¼ tsp cumin powder
  • ½ garam masala
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cream
  • 16 cubes paneer
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • 2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. How to make gravy base:
  2. 1.Firstly, in a large kadhai heat 2 tbsp oil and 2 tbsp butter.
  3. 2.Add 5 cloves, 2 pods cardamom and sauté on low flame until it turns aromatic.
  4. 3.Now add 2 onion, 2 clove garlic, 1 inch ginger.
  5. 4.Sauté until onions shrink slightly.
  6. 5.Further add 3 tomato, 15 cashew and sauté for 2 minutes.
  7. 6.Cover and boil for 10 minutes, or until tomato softens.
  8. 7.Cool completely, transfer to the blender and blend to smooth paste.
  9. 8.Pass the curry to the filter to get rid of seeds and skin.
  10. 9.Smooth and silky, the tomato-onion paste is ready. keep aside.
  11. How to make paneer butter masala:
  12. 1.Firstly, in a large kadhai heat 2 tbsp oil, 2 tbsp butter, 1 bay leaf and 1 chilli. sauté until the spices turn aromatic.
  13. 2.Now add 1 onion and sauté until onions turn and golden brown.
  14. 3.Keeping the flame on low, add ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, ¾ tsp coriander powder, ¼ tsp cumin powder, ½ garam masala and 1 tsp salt.
  15. 4.Sauté until the spices turn aromatic. make sure not to burn the spices.
  16. 5.Add in prepared onion tomato paste and mix well.
  17. 6.Cook until the oil separates from the masala paste.
  18. 7.Now add 1 cup water and mix well adjusting the consistency.
  19. 8.Further, add 2 tbsp cream and mix well.
  20. 9.Also, add 16 cubes of paneer and mix gently.
  21. 10.Simmer for 5 minutes, or until the paneer absorbs the flavour.
  22. 11.Add 1 tsp kasuri methi and 2 tbsp coriander. mix well.
  23. 12.Finally, enjoy paneer butter masala with roti or naan
